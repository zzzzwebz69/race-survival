Race Survival: Arena King

Get ready to take the driver's seat in Race Survival: Arena King, a high-octane racing game that challenges your survival skills on a precarious, ever-changing hexagonal racetrack! Compete against other racers, dodging and weaving through tiles that disappear beneath you, and strive to be the last one standing by knocking your opponents off the track into the abyss.

How to play Race Survival: Arena King?

Your ultimate goal is to outlast all other vehicles in the arena with a hexagonal-tiled surface. Each match begins with your vehicle automatically accelerating forward as soon as you enter the arena. Your task is to maneuver your car towards the opponents and make strategic jumps to bump them off into the water below.

Remember to collect coins scattered on the track; these are crucial for unlocking new, faster sports cars. Also, keep an eye on specific tasks listed in the menu to unlock additional vehicles. There are several game modes in Race Survival: Arena King each offering unique challenges and objectives:


  • Practice Mode: Free racing with no opponents to get you used to the games' physics and controls
  • Battle Mode: Focus on survival and make your car jump and fly to avoid falling into the sea
  • Parkour Mode: Collect the most stars while navigating the same treacherous track
  • Autoball Mode: Aim to score the most points by kicking balls into a net, without falling off the hexagons.

What are the controls for Race Survival: Arena King?

Desktops:

  • A and D/Left and Right Arrows: To steer
  • Upper Arrow/W/Spacebar: To jump
  • Tap Up Arrow/W/Spacebar twice: To fly
  • Left click can also be used to jump and fly
  • Joystick compatible


Mobile Devices:

  • Touchscreen controls are used to steer, jump, and fly.

Features

  • Challenging survival driving gameplay on a racetrack made of hexagons
  • Vehicle upgrades and customization through collected coins
  • Crazy track environment with disappearing tiles
